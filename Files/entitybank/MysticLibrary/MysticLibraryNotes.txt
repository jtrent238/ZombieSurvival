The textures in this modelpack use media from CGTextures. Visit www.cgtextures.com for more information.

Models marked with HP are higher in polycount for their size. This means that they have more
geometrical detail and inform leveldesigners that they shouldn't use them as often in a map as other models.

All Models have specular maps designed for FPSCX9 - Metro theater pack - lightmap_herospec.fx
shader effect.
